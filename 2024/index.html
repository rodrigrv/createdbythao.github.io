About

Who's Thao?

The Professional Answer:
Thao is a freelance graphic designer in Arlington, Texas who specializes in print design with experience in digital design with the belief of less is more unless it's plain. She has a BFA in Visual Communications from the University of Texas at Arlington in 2016.

The Casual Answer:
Thao is a graphic designer who likes print because she can physically touch the paper and is learning to like the web since it's almost essential and a nice puzzle to figure out.

In her spare time, she likes to read (mainly fantasy books), nap and garden (only weeds are growing). Her family consists of a husband, child, 3 cats and one dog.
